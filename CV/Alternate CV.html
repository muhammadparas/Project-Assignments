Mission & Vission

I am just going to copy this from interenet as it is more valid and authentic sounding. This mission statement logically combines the key elements of Aristotle’s virtue ethics, Bentham’s consequential ethics, Kant’s deontological ethics and Socrates existentialism.

In simple terms: those activities that optimally make us happy and which we perform with excellence, but at the same time constrained by moral principles (with respect to other people), will lead to the maximum increase in our self-knowledge. Which is the main reason we all came to Earth.

If our activities do not make us happy or we do not perform them excellently, our self-learning is less. Also if we ignore moral principles, we enter into tension or possibly conflict with others (or in worst case end up in prison or dead)… which also takes us away from those happy and excellent activities which most improve our self-knowledge
